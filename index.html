Storybooks Pakistan

Storybooks Pakistan is a free open educational resource that promotes literacy and language learning in homes, schools, and communities. It makes 40 stories from the African Storybook available with text and audio in Urdu, English, and the most widely spoken languages of Pakistan. Share and enjoy!
